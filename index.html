About

A Sound That Takes You Back

Jeff Rogers brings the golden era of American music to life — solo on guitar with rich professional backing tracks that fill the room. If your guests grew up with the Rat Pack, the British Invasion, Motown, or classic rock, they’ll feel right at home.

Based in the Charlotte area, Jeff performs roughly 70–75 events each year across the region, building a reputation for showing up prepared, playing non-stop, and reading a room perfectly.
